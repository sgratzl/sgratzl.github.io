About

Samuel Gratzl is a passionate information visualizer and experienced software architect. He loves to dig into code, hunt bugs, and design new platforms. In addition, he teaches Information Visualization classes at universities.

He is currently on sabbatical in Melbourne, Australia looking for a new challenge in his life. He co-founded the spin-off company datavisyn GmbH in 2016. datavisyn develops data visualization solutions for applications in pharmaceutical and biomedical R&D.
